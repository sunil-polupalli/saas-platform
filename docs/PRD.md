# Product Requirements Document

## User Personas
1. **Super Admin:** Manages the system.
2. **Tenant Admin:** Manages their organization.
3. **User:** Works on projects and tasks.

## Functional Requirements
1. Tenant Registration with unique subdomain.
2. JWT-based Authentication.
3. Strict Data Isolation.
4. Project Creation & Listing.
5. Task Management (CRUD).
6. Team Member Management (Invite/Add).
